I'm Eric, and I've been obsessed with electronic circuits practically ever since I was born. When I was little, I would play around with alligator clips and batteries and build simple circuits. Once, I built a vibration motor into a metal can and made a "rattle machine!" One Saturday morning, we were looking on FaceBook for nearby yard sales and found out a family was moving and were giving away many things for free. Enthused, we went to check it out. Now, this was a while ago, so I may not recall things perfectly, but as I remember it, there was a giant dumpster in their yard that my dad began sifting through, He found tons of electronic components. Alongside trash, he discovered Raspberry Pis, Arduinos, various components, and even an MP3 player! The man who owned the stuff saw that we were interested in the electronic-type stuff, so he went inside, and later came back with giant bins of various electronic things in tow. There was an oscilloscope, a function generator, two RF signal generators, and more! If you've ever watched my retro oscilloscope video, retro RF generator video, or old lab tech lot video, now you know where I got the equipment from! He also gave us a touchscreen monitor with a RasPi 2B and got us another monitor with a RasPi 3B inside! There was so much, in fact, we had to make two or three trips from our house and back just to fit it in our car! (And it wasn't a small car, either!) These supplies helped me pursue my love for electronics further and further, and as I learned what more and more of the stuff actually was (yes, most of it I had no idea what it was), I figured out how to use it and as I got older, began researching and learning a ton! Thanks to the internet, I was able to teach myself everything I know about electronics today! (Or most of it, anyway.) Another great interest I had was making videos! When I was younger, I received a camera for Christmas and began filming my own vlogs. I desperately wanted a YouTube channel of my own, but my parents wouldn't allow it. I promised myself that when I became old enough to, I would post my early videos, and... well... obviously I haven't. Nowadays I enjoy creating my videos and sharing my passion with the world. Lately, I have also taken an interest in radio and enjoy using SDRs to listen to all sorts of stations. I hope you enjoy my channel and everything it has to offer!
